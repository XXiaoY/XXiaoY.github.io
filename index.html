Xiaohan Yuan

I am a Lecturer at the School of Software, Henan Polytechnic University (HPU), working with Prof. Xixi Yan (闫玺玺) and Prof. Yongli Tang (汤永利) . Before this, I received my Ph.D. degree in 2025 from the School of Cyberspace Science and Technology at Beijing Jiaotong University, supervised by Prof. Jiqiang Liu (刘吉强) and Prof. Wei Wang (王伟) .

My current research interests include Cryptography, Blockchain Security, and AI Security.
